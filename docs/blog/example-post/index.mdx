## 8. Math Equations (KaTeX)

You can write beautiful math equations using KaTeX:

### Inline Math

Write inline math by wrapping LaTeX equations between `$`:

Let $f\colon[a,b]\to\mathbb{R}$ be Riemann integrable. Let $F\colon[a,b]\to\mathbb{R}$ be $F(x)=\int_{a}^{x} f(t)\,dt$. Then $F$ is continuous, and at all $x$ such that $f$ is continuous at $x$, $F$ is differentiable at $x$ with $F'(x)=f(x)$.


### Block Math

For display mode equations, use `$$`:

$$
I = \int_0^{2\pi} \sin(x)\,dx
$$

$$
E = mc^2
$$
